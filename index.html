Abstract

This paper presents a proof-of-concept for a decentralized machine learning framework utilizing blockchain technology. By integrating Python (Hardhat, Solidity, PyTorch) and Rust (Substrate, WebAssembly, Burn), we demonstrate the feasibility of conducting both training and inference directly on-chain. Our approach leverages advanced techniques such as inter-layer and intra-layer model splits, Distributed Data Parallelism (DDP), and Pipeline Parallelism. We also propose a novel Proof of Learning (PoL) mechanism based on zk-SNARKs to validate training results while maintaining privacy.
